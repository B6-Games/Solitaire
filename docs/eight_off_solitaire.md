## Rules of Eight Off Solitaire

### üÉè Objective
Move all cards to the **foundations**, building **Ace ‚Üí King** in the same suit for each of the four suits.

---

### üé¥ Setup
- Use **one 52-card deck**.
- Deal the **tableau** into **eight columns**:
  - **6 cards** per column **face up** (total 48 cards).
- Place the remaining **4 cards** face up into **four of the eight reserve cells** (the other **4 cells start empty**).
- Foundations start empty; Aces will be moved there as they appear.

> **At a glance:** 8 tableau columns, **8 reserve cells** (4 filled, 4 empty), build to foundations by suit.

### üîÅ Gameplay

1. **Tableau Building (By Suit)**
   - Build **down in rank and same suit** (e.g., ‚ô†8 on ‚ô†9).
   - You may move a **single card** or a **properly ordered in-suit sequence** if your empty cells/columns allow it (see Mobility).

2. **Reserve Cells (‚ÄúEight Off‚Äù)**
   - Each cell holds **one card**.
   - Use cells as **temporary storage** to free and realign tableau cards.
   - Try to keep some cells **open**; empty cells are your mobility engine.

3. **Empty Columns**
   - Only a **King** (or a **sequence starting with a King**) may be moved into an empty tableau column.

4. **Foundations**
   - Move **Aces** to foundations when available, then build up by suit to **King**.
